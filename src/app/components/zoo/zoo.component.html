Welcome to the Zoo!

This project was created with the intention of demonstrating the power of complex Angular reactive forms.

A zoo is made up of Environments. Each Environment can contain 0-n Zones. Each Zone can contain 0-n Animals. Animals are of a certain Species and are either herbivores or carnivores.

If you house an adult herbivore with an adult carnivore, the carnivore will eat the herbivore.

Juvenile carnivores will not eat other animals.
